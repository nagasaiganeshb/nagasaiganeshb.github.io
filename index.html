Full Story

From Machilipatnam
to the AI frontier.

A proper account of the journey — written so the context never gets lost.

Chapter 1 · 2000–2015
The Spark — Machilipatnam

Naga Sai Ganesh was born on 4th July 2000 in Machilipatnam — a coastal town in Andhra Pradesh known for its kalamkari textiles. Growing up in a middle-class family, access to technology was not a given — but encouragement was.

His parents pushed their own boundaries. When they saw his curiosity, they got him a computer. They tapped their network — a colleague like a brother to his father shared how to participate in technical competitions. Not wealthy connections. People passing on knowledge.

The first real encounter with programming came at Kendriya Vidyalaya, Machilipatnam. HTML in the curriculum. For most students, just another subject. For him, a revelation. Around the summer of 8th standard, a colleague of his father taught him C programming. Not a class — just a curious kid and someone who cared enough to show him. He was around 13.

Chapter 2 · 2015–2018
The Foundation — Polytechnic, Gudlavalleru

AP POLYCET Rank 49,458. AANM & VVRSR Polytechnic, Gudlavalleru. What happened over three years defied that rank at every step. Year 1: deep in C and architecture. By end of Year 1: section topper. He and his friends had a daily ritual — waiting for the bus, talking. One day: traffic. Ambulances stuck at signals. That conversation sparked a question: what if the ambulance could communicate with the signal itself?

He built the Smart Ambulance — MCS-51 microcontroller, IR sensors, Assembly Language. Not for marks. Because the problem felt real.

Competition trail (Year 2–3): Bannari Amman Institute, TN (1st · IoT) → DKTE Polytechnic, Ichalkaranji (loss → lesson in how to present) → CSESTA 2018 (2nd + Special Appreciation, one day prep) → Techno-Innova, Bharati Vidyapeeth Pune (2nd Paper · 1st C · 1st Java, competing against engineering students)

Final: 96.94% · 2nd Rank, entire Computer Engineering dept, both shifts. The Prathibha Award 2018 was presented by CM Shri N. Chandrababu Naidu — a state honour for a kid from Machilipatnam whose parents had stretched for a computer years before.

Chapter 3 · 2018–2021
The University Years — Acharya Nagarjuna University

AP ECET Rank 29 in Computer Science. Lateral entry into ANU, Guntur. Found the Innovation Cell and built Team Evolvers — CSE, ECE, Mechanical. Weekend hackathons. LeetCode. HackerRank. Every new environment showed the gap between where he was and where he could be. That gap was fuel.

In 2019, floods hit 13 Indian states. 300+ lives lost. Team Evolvers built an answer: Smart Rescue System — Python CV on Raspberry Pi, person detection on live FPV drone feed, GPS-GSM transmission to ground units.

IISF 2019, Kolkata: Jointly organised by Union Ministries of Science & Technology, Earth Sciences, and Health. Supporting: DST, CSIR, ISRO, DRDO. 12,000+ participants. Inaugurated by PM Narendra Modi via video conferencing. Team Evolvers: Runner-up, Strategic Applications.

Then the goal since 2019: Smart India Hackathon 2020. Problem MK93, Govt. of Uttarakhand. Cleared all rounds. COVID delayed the finale. Remote coordination. And — National Winners, SIH 2020 Hardware Edition. B.Tech completed September 2021. CGPA 8.0. Over 10 MNC offers — all turned down. Chose startups.

Chapter 4 · Jan 2022 – Present
MSRcosmos — Building at Scale

Joined MSRcosmos as Software Engineer, January 2022. Four years of progressively deeper impact.

OnlineSilver (2022) — Greenfield. Full lifecycle. Live at onlinesilver.com. Built a reusable component library still used across projects — 50% faster feature delivery.

Vinci360 (Late 2022) — A retired Django system revived in one week for a demo, then scaled into vinci360.com: 200% scalability, 1M+ records/day, Azure OpenAI analytics.

CrowdPulse (2023) — Same CV foundation from Smart Rescue System, now at enterprise scale: real-time crowd emotion from 50+ simultaneous streams, 75% latency reduction.

MSR GenAI (2023–24) — Multi-tenant AI SaaS. RAG: 90% accuracy. 80% dev cost reduction. $3,600 annual savings.

Agentic System for eGovernance (2024–Present) — Production RAG for government analysts. Active R&D into hybrid retrieval, contextual chunking, re-ranking.

AI Companion — Bridging AI across the organisation: Oracle 26AI, Copilot Studio, Azure AI Foundry, Power Apps.

The Thread
Observation → Problem → Build

Smart Ambulance: a bus-stop conversation. Smart Rescue: watching flood news. CrowdPulse: the same CV core that found people in floods, now reading emotions in crowds.

"Experiencing" — Direct engagement over passive learning. "Evolving" — Team Evolvers. Team Vyakta. Growth is continuous. Direction matters more than speed.
